2023 Solution Challenge

The 2023 Solution Challenge will launch event will begin on January 11, 2023 from 11 am!

The mission of the 2023 Solution Challenge is to solve for one or more of the United Nations 17 Sustainable Development Goals. The Solution Challenge is open to members of Google Developer Student Clubs.
